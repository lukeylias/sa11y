Ensure text contrast

What is text contrast?

Text contrast refers to the difference in lightness and color between text and its background. Sufficient contrast makes text readable for users with visual impairments, including those with color blindness or low vision.

Best practices

Ensuring proper text contrast is crucial for making your content accessible to all users. Here are some best practices to help you achieve this:

  • Use a color contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1

    This ratio is the minimum requirement for normal text and a contrast ratio of 7:1 for large text.

  • Avoid using color alone to convey information

    Use text labels or icons in addition to color to convey information. This ensures that users with color blindness can understand the content.

  • Choose color schemes that naturally provide high contrast

    Selecting color combinations that inherently offer high contrast helps in maintaining readability across different elements of your design.

  • Implement CSS styles that enforce minimum contrast ratios

    Use CSS to set and enforce contrast ratios. This helps ensure that the design maintains the required contrast levels consistently.

  • Avoid placing text over complex backgrounds

    Text overlaid on images or complex patterns can become unreadable. Ensure that text is placed on solid backgrounds or areas with sufficient contrast.

How to test

  • Use automated tools

    Automated tools like the WebAIM Contrast Checker or the Axe Accessibility Checker can quickly evaluate the contrast ratio of your text and background colors. These tools provide instant feedback and suggest improvements if the contrast is insufficient.

  • Manually check contrast ratios

    Manual checking involves using color contrast analyzers to verify the contrast ratio between text and background colors. Tools like the Paciello Group’s Color Contrast Analyser allow you to manually input color values and ensure they meet the required standards.

  • Test under different lighting conditions

    Testing text contrast under various lighting conditions ensures that your content remains readable in different environments. View your content in bright sunlight, dim lighting, and typical office settings to check for consistent readability.

  • Test on various devices

    Ensure your text contrast is effective across different devices and screen sizes. Use smartphones, tablets, and desktop monitors to verify that text remains readable and maintains adequate contrast on all platforms.
